PRECAUTIONS


FOR UNDERWEIGHT:

If your BMI range is 17 or less than 17 kg/m2(Underweight).

Risks associated with being Underweight:

  • A decrease in immune function
  • Osteoporosis, a disease that causes bone weakness, increasing the risk of breaking a bone
  • Growth and development issues, particularly in children and teenagers

Solution:

  • Using healthy fats like olive oil or avocado
  • Adding skim milk powder to soups, stews and drinks
  • Adding protein powder to milkshakes
  • Doing some exercise to increase your appetite

FOR OVERWEIGHT:

If your BMI range is 25 or above 25 kg/m2(Overweight or obesity).

Risks associated with being Overweight:

  • High blood pressure
  • Low quality of life
  • Body pains and difficulty with certain physical functions
  • Mental illnesses such as clinical depression, anxiety, and others

Solution:

  • Eat a wide variety of vegetables, fruits, grains, proteins and reduced-fat dairy foods each day.
  • Drink water instead of sugary drinks such as soft drinks and fruit juice.
  • Make the most of your local bush track, bike path, playground or sports field.
  • Focus on steps towards a healthy lifestyle rather than setting weight-loss goals.
